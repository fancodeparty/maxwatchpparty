How to Elevate Your Viewing Experience with a Max Watch Party?

Planning a Max Party is effortless with these straightforward steps. The Max Watch Party extension is designed to be user-friendly, ensuring accessibility for all. Moreover, follow these simple steps to get started and enjoy your favorite shows and movies together in no time

  • Max Watch Party Installation Firstly, click here to install the Max Watch Party extension. This extension is compatible with Chromebooks, macOS, and Windows devices and supports Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge browsers.
  • Pin the Extension to Your Toolbar Pinning the Max Watch Party Chrome extension to your toolbar will ensure it is easily accessible. To do so, navigate to the "Extensions" icon at the top right corner of your browser. And then, click the pin button next to the Max Watch Party extension.
  • Account Sign-In Sign in to your Max Party account to join or create a watch party and enjoy your favorite shows and movies with friends.
  • Play the Video After logging into your Max Party account, search for the video you wish to watch. Once you’ve found it, start playing the video to enjoy it with your watch party.
  • Launch Your Party Click the “PLAY” button in the toolbar to host a watch party. This will open the “Create a Party” window, where you can select the “Start a Party” option. Moreover, decide if you want to control the watch party by toggling the “Only I have control” button on or off. A watch party URL will be generated, which you can share with your friends to invite them to join the party.
  • Join the Party First, install the extension and log in to your Max Party account to join a Max Party. Then, click the Watch Party URL shared by your friend to enter the party and enjoy the show together.
